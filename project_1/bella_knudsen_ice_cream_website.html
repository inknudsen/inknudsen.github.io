Top 8 Ice Cream Shops in Central Pennsylvania

Whether it's a treat after a day out with the family or the perfect ending to a hike with your pup, ice cream is a delicious choice of snack. And there is no shortage of fantastic choices for a scoop of your favorite flavor in Central Pennsylvania. This list compiles our top 8 choices for getting that cold summer treat. If you are looking for something in particular don’t forget to check out the key below to quickly find that perfect ice cream stop.

Mount Gretna, PA 17064

Closed

A super popular ice cream shop nestled in the heart of the Mount Gretna village. A lovely view of the nearby rustic buildings and wooded area makes for a relaxing afternoon experience after a day at the nearby lake or roller skating rink. The menu includes fanciful ice cream sundaes and a variety of foods.
